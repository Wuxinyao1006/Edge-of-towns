Welcome to Edge of Towns

Welcome To Our Motel

Welcome to our cozy motel set in the rural beauty of Door County! Our motel has been thoughtfully remodeled, with each room featuring two beds, comfortably accommodating up to four guests. Start your day with our complimentary breakfast items and enjoy a fresh start.

Open year-round with convenient access to explore the charming attractions of Door County and the northern Sister Bay. Whether you're here to relax or seek adventure, we look forward to providing you with an unforgettable countryside experience!

Our rooms are thoughtfully equipped for home-like comfort, with high-speed internet, cable TV, a private bathroom, mini fridge, microwave, table and chairs, luggage rack, and coffee maker. We ensure convenience and comfort for every guest.

Accessible pathways and walk-in showers provide ease for all visitors. Outdoor enthusiasts will enjoy a range of activities, from fishing and kayaking to biking, barbecues, and picnics. Our facilities include a barbecue area, terrace, picnic spots, fire pit, horseshoe and bocce courts, and over 20 games to ensure a fun and memorable stay..
