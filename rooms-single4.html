Luxury Room - (Standard Room With 2 Beds)

The Edge of Town Waterfall Inn is a one story authentic Door County Motel that has been recently remodeled. We are located right off of highway 42 just 1 mile north of downtown Sister bay, offering a tranquil, relaxing atmosphere. If you're looking to have easy access to all that Sister Bay offers but would like to stay out of the "tension zone" then this is the perfect place for you.

  • Max: 4 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 2

This spacious room has two full sized beds with a new walk in shower, wifi, cable TV, heat, air conditioning, front door parking, new flooring, and updated bathroom vanities. It also has a kitchenette which includes a microwave, refrigerator, dining table, regular and Keurig coffee makers, hair dryer, linens, towels, and soap!

We offer a variety of amenities to make your stay with us as enjoyable as possible. We have ample parking for cars, boats, and trailers including outdoor electric plug ins. We offer complementary grab and go breakfast and coffee in our breakfast room that you can enjoy inside, on the go, or on our outdoor patio! The grounds also include a picnic area and charcoal grills are provided as well.

$125.00 plus tax
